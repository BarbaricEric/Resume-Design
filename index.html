About Me

Hello

My name is Eric and I'm interested in a position where I am able to hone my programming, animation, and art abilities. I have always had a strong desire to know more about how humans interact with each other and with their surroundings. This aspiration has led me to acquire a beginners level knowledge/skills necessary to dabble in the research, design, and development field through internships and educational pursuits.
